Welcome to Percepta

Percepta is an advanced real-time violence detection system designed to enhance security and safety through AI-powered surveillance. Using state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning, Percepta continuously monitors live video feeds, detecting violent incidents such as fights, assaults, and physical altercations with high accuracy.

Once an incident is identified, Percepta instantly triggers alerts, providing security teams and law enforcement with real-time notifications, timestamps, and video evidence. This enables faster response times and proactive intervention, reducing risks and improving situational awareness.
